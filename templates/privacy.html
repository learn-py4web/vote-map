Terms of Use

By using Vote Map, you are agreeing with the privacy policy below.

Furthermore, if you edit the map, you agree to provide correct and up to date information.

Privacy Policy

Cookies

Vote-Map uses cookies to enable you to log in into the site, and authenticate you if you visit again. When you log in, Vote-Map stores your email address, which is used to identify you in the system.

Editing the site

When you edit the site, Vote-Map stores the data of your edit (the location where ballots can be returned, and the associated data), along with your identity, and the time when the edit was done.

Logs

Vote-Map logs all accesses. For every access, the IP address of the originator is logged, along with the user email address if the user is logged in; details of the HTTP request are also logged. These logs are retained for some time, typically about a week. The logs are used to debug errors in the website and profile its performance.

Use of third-party infrastructure

Vote-Map is hosted on the Google Cloud, and we use Google Cloud services to store the data and to display the maps.

Sharing your personal information

Vote-Map does not share your personal information. Vote-Map shares the data on polling site locations that you and others entered with its users, and possibly with third parties.
