Vote-Map
Searching ballot return locations

Vote-Map enables you to look up the locations where you can return your ballots without having to email them. To search the locations, enter your zip code. The reason we allow you to look up locations only by zip code, rather than by browsing a map, is to reduce our costs, as we can precompute the results for each zip code.

Editing ballot return locations

The locations returned by Vote-Map are crowsourced. To edit the locations, you need to log in and follow the instructions in the edit and invite tabs that will appear in the top bar.
